Salad is Made using Chickpick

Everyone Should Eat Salad in morning

Ingrediants

  • Chickpick
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Salt
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Wash the Chickpicks
  2. Cut the Tomato and Onion in small small pieces
  3. Mix everything in one Bowl
  4. Add Lemon And Salt And Enjoy.
